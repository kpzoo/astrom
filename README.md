Reproduces all analyses and figures from the paper "Epidemic indicators do not determine intervention performance" by Kris V Parag.
Generates two counterexamples (loosely inspired by Karl Astrom's textbook). We consider 3 indicators: growth rates, reproduction numbers and the curve of new infections. Key results show:

1) [run counterFig1.m] Two epidemics with indistinguishable indicators pre-intervention that diverge due to feedback control.
2) [run counterFig2.m] Two epidemics with different indicators pre-intervention that converge due to feedback control.
